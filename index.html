A tiny tale for July 7th

One Night a Year七夕

Orihime the weaver and Hikoboshi the cowherd fell so deeply in love that all their work was forgotten. The Sky King parted them across the Heavenly River — the Milky Way — allowing them to meet only on the seventh night of the seventh month.

Tonight is that night. Help them find their way back to each other.

The lovers are reunited

Make a Wish短冊

On Tanabata, wishes are written on paper strips called tanzaku and hung on bamboo, so the stars may carry them.
